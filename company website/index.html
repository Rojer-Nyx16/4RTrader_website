About Us

Strategically situated in the SIDCO Industrial Estate, Vadakkuvalliyur, 4R Trader specializes exclusively in the manufacturing of high-quality Aluminium Ingots. The company focuses on producing precision-crafted products that meet the stringent standards of the metals industry. Driven by a core commitment to purity, innovation, and customer satisfaction, 4R Trader ensures rigorous adherence to quality control measures and regulatory requirements throughout its operations. The manufacturing unit is equipped with essential tools and processes necessary for consistent and reliable Aluminium Ingots production, assuring clients of both product integrity and consistency.
